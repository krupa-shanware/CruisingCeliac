Saraso ka Saag and Makki ki Roti

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of mustard leaves
  • 1 cup spinach
  • GF spices
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Tomato paste
  • GF corn or makki flour(we use MASECA Amarillo Instant Yellow Corn Masa Flour)

Directions

Saag

Cut mustard seed leaves into small pieces along with the stems. Cut half of the spinach.

Put the cut mustard leaves and spinach pot with a little oil. Add 2 pieces of ginger, salt, and steam.

Coarsely grind the mixture.

Add oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds for tadka, cut onion, chili, and ginger garlic paste.

Fry for a few minutes, then add some tomato paste. Also add chili powder, turmeric and salt.

Add the green mix and cook it well.Add some butter and let it cook, the saag might be slightly soggy.

Now add 2 spoons of corn flour, mix, and cook again with a lid.On top add hot oil tadka with garlic and mustard seeds.

Makki Rotti

Boil 2 cups of water with a little salt and a little ghee.

Add corn flour, turn off the gas, and mix. Cover for 10 minutes.

Take the flour mix, put it on a plate, and make dough by adding some water.

Make small balls and roll to make roti. Flip on flame directly. Roast with some ghee for added softness.

Serve together and enjoy your meal!
