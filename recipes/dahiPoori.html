Dahi Poori

Ingredients

  • Poori from Pani Poori
  • Sweet chutney
  • Mint chutney
  • Yogurt
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Shev(fried chickpeas snack)
  • Mint
  • Garnish: boiled potatoes, cilantro, onion, green chillies, and pomegranate seeds for garnishing

Directions

Arrange 8-10 pani pooris in a plate.

Mix 1 cup yogurt with 2 teaspoons of sugar and a pinch of salt.

Make a hole in each poori and stuff each poori with potato, onion, green chutney, and sweet chutney.

Drizzle with yogurt. Sprinkle cilantro, cut green chillies, and pomegranate seeds on top. Top with crunchy shev.

Enjoy Dahi Poori as a snack!
