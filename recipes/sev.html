Shev-Sev Recipe (Indian Namkeen snack)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup besan or chickpea flour (Bob’s Red Mill or Vitacost GF Chickpea Flour)
  • 1/4 cup rice flour (Bob’s Red Mill Or Vitacost GF Rice Flour)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • a pinch of baking soda
  • 1/3 cup water
  • sev or chaki sacha for making sev or chakli

Directions

Add besan and rice flour into a mixing bowl.

To that add salt, turmeric powder, baking soda, and mix well.

Add water to make it into a soft dough. Cover and allow it to sit for 10 minutes. While it sits, preheat oil for frying into the seperate kadhai.

Now divide sev dough into two equal portions. Prepare chakali mold by putting fine sev attachment in it, and put sev dough in chakali mold.

Press the dough into hot oil in a circular motion, making sure the heat is at medium temperature. Deep fry intil it gets crisp.

Drain off all excess oil from sev. Allow the sev to cool off, then store it into an airtight container.

Enjoy with Bhel or pani poori, dahi poori, or just by itself!
