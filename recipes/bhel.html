Cruising Celiac

Bhel

Ingredients

  • Puffed rice or murmura
  • Poha
  • Green chutney
  • Sweet chutney
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Green chillies
  • Mango
  • Shev(fried chickpies snack) or GF chivda mix

Directions

Cut onion, tomatoes, raw mango, chillies, and cilantro into small pieces.

Mix with 2 cups of puffed rice, 2 cups of poha, 2 cups of GF chivda mix, green chutney, and sweet chutney.

Serve immediately and enjoy!
